Nº 01 — The Studio

About the studio

We create celebrations that feel deeply personal, visually distinctive, and effortless for every guest.

Élan Events is a boutique planning and design studio for people who care how things feel. We take on a small number of weddings and celebrations each year — never two at once — so every event receives the studio's full attention.

From first conversation to last dance, we design and produce every detail: the place, the light, the table, the music, the pace of the evening. Our clients tend to describe the result the same way — like the best night of their lives, only calmer.

Nº 03 — Featured Celebration

A Mediterranean Weddingin Mallorca

Claire & Thomas — three days in the Serra de Tramuntana, June 2025.

Eighty-six guests flew in from eleven countries. What they found was not a schedule but a rhythm: a citrus-grove dinner to arrive to, vows beneath thousand-year olives, and a courtyard that refused to let the evening end.

Location
Serra de Tramuntana, Mallorca
Guests

Event type

Creative direction

Planning

01 — The Welcome A citrus grove, lanterns in the branches, first embraces

02 — The Ceremony

Vows beneath thousand-year olives

No aisle runner, no arch for the sake of an arch — just dry-stone walls, dappled light and chairs set close enough that every guest could hear the vows without a microphone.

The courtyard glowed until two, and nobody reached for their coat.

05 — The Evening Dinner under festoon lights, toasts in three languages

06 — The Last Dance

Nº 05 — The Approach

Five stages.One calm surface.

Rigour underneath, ease on top. This is how a celebration stays effortless.

Discovery01 / 05

01

Discovery

We begin with conversation, not concepts. Who you are, how you host, the moments you want to keep forever — and the ones you'd rather skip. From this we shape a clear brief and an honest budget.

Listening · Brief · Budget

02

Creative Direction

One narrative told through everything: place, palette, paper, flowers, food, light and music. We present a single considered direction — then refine it with you until it feels inevitable.

Narrative · Palette · Design

03

Planning & Production

Venues held, artisans booked, logistics mapped to the minute. You see a calm surface; beneath it sits a production schedule that accounts for everything — including the weather.

Venues · Artisans · Logistics

04

Guest Experience

We design the celebration from your guests' point of view: arrivals, rooms, translations, children, chairs in the shade. Guests who feel cared for make the best memories.

Arrival · Comfort · Flow

05

The Event Day

Our team leads the day so no one else has to. Timings, weather calls, quiet corrections — handled invisibly. You are, at last, a guest at your own celebration.

Direction · Calm · Presence

Nº 07 — The Founder

Amelia Laurent

Founder & Creative Director

Amelia spent seven years producing fashion shows and editorial shoots between Paris and London — until a friend's wedding on the Amalfi Coast quietly changed the direction of everything. She founded Élan in 2016 with a simple conviction: a celebration can be produced with the rigour of a couture show and still feel like the warmest evening of your life.

She remains personally involved in every event the studio takes on — obsessive about light, honest about budgets, and usually the first to arrive on site. Clients tend to arrive as clients and leave as friends who call her first with good news.

A great celebration isn't about more. It's one clear feeling, held from the first glass to the last dance.
