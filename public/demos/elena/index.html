01 Philosophy

I do not redesign faces. I work with skin, proportion and time — preserving the person already there.

Every face carries its own logic — bone, muscle, skin, habit, history. My work is to read that logic and support it, never to overwrite it.

The result is skin that behaves well, and a face that still looks like you — on your best day.

05 The specialist

Elena, in person.

Elena Orlova is an independent aesthetic cosmetologist working at the meeting point of dermatology-informed skin care and aesthetic judgement. Her practice is deliberately small: one room, one specialist, unhurried appointments — and protocols built around skin health rather than trends.

She works with what a face already is — its structure, its ageing pattern, its character — and treats “natural” not as a style, but as the standard of a job done well.
