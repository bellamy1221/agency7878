Material library

Twelve materials. One palette.

We would rather do twelve materials properly than two hundred badly. Each is chosen to age well, repair well and sit quietly next to the others — so any combination you configure still reads as one room.

fabric

Washed Linen

A dry, matte weave that softens with use. The honest default — cool in summer, quietly textural all year.

Composition
100% European flax, stonewashed
Origin
Woven in Guimarães, Portugal
Care
Removable covers, 30° gentle wash

Used on: Aria, Otta, Nube, Notte

fabric

Bouclé

Looped yarn with a soft, cloud-like relief. Forgiving in daily use and beautiful in raking light.

Composition
62% wool, 30% polyamide, 8% cotton
Origin
Milled in Prato, Italy
Care
Vacuum with soft brush; professional clean

Used on: Aria, Otta, Nube, Notte

fabric

Felted Wool

Dense, springy and long-wearing. The upholstery equivalent of a good winter coat.

Composition
95% new wool, 5% polyamide
Origin
Kvadrat-grade mill, Innvik, Norway
Care
Brush regularly; spot clean with wool soap

Used on: Aria, Otta, Nube, Notte

fabric

Cotton Velvet

A short, dense pile that holds light like evening sun on plaster. Use it where you want warmth.

Composition
88% cotton, 12% modal pile
Origin
Woven in Como, Italy
Care
Steam to lift pile; professional clean

Used on: Aria, Otta, Nube, Notte

fabric

Aniline Leather

Uncorrected hide that patinas rather than wears. Every scratch becomes part of the record.

Composition
Full-grain aniline hide, vegetable tanned
Origin
Tanned in Tuscany, Italy
Care
Condition twice yearly; keep out of direct sun

Used on: Aria, Otta

wood

Pale Oak

Light, even grain finished with hardwax oil. The backbone of the collection.

Composition
FSC European oak, natural oil
Origin
Jutland, Denmark
Care
Re-oil yearly; wipe with damp cloth

Used on: Piano, Scala, Basso, Desco

wood

Smoked Oak

Oak darkened to the core, not stained on the surface. Reads as bronze in low light.

Composition
FSC European oak, ammonia smoked
Origin
Jutland, Denmark
Care
Re-oil yearly; wipe with damp cloth

Used on: Piano, Scala, Basso, Desco

wood

Walnut

Warm chocolate figure with occasional pale sap streaks. Kept for tops and fronts.

Composition
FSC American black walnut, natural oil
Origin
Pennsylvania, USA
Care
Re-oil yearly; avoid standing water

Used on: Piano, Scala, Basso, Desco

stone

Limestone

Fossil-flecked and soft grey-beige. Honed, never polished — light lands on it gently.

Composition
Honed Jura limestone, 20 mm
Origin
Bavaria, Germany
Care
Seal yearly; wipe spills promptly

Used on: Piano, Desco

stone

Travertine

Banded, porous, ancient. The most Italian thing we make furniture out of.

Composition
Filled Roman travertine, 20 mm
Origin
Tivoli, Italy
Care
Seal yearly; use coasters

Used on: Piano

metal

Cool, precise, quietly industrial. Used sparingly — legs, frames, pulls.

Composition
Brushed stainless steel, clear coat
Origin
Brescia, Italy

Wipe with microfibre; no abrasives

A soft-touch, low-sheen surface in architectural colours. Calm and easy to live with.
