About Stanza

Stanza is the Italian word for room — and the unit we design in.

We started in a Stockholm apartment with a sofa that didn’t fit and a tape measure that didn’t lie. Seven years later we make modular furniture across five European workshops, sold one configuration at a time. Nothing we produce exists until someone composes it in a real room.

01

The room decides

We start from floor plans, door widths and the way afternoon light moves — then design furniture that adapts to them, not the other way round.

02

Made when ordered

No warehouses of guesses. Your configuration opens a work order in a named workshop, and you can follow it from cut list to crate.

03

Built to be repaired

Covers unzip, modules unbolt, parts stay available for fifteen years. The most sustainable sofa is the one you never replace.
