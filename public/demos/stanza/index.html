06Craft & materials

Made slowly, in five workshops we can name.

Frames are joined in Jutland, covers cut in Prato, stone honed outside Rome. Nothing is built for stock — your configuration starts a work order, not a warehouse search. That is why lead times are honest weeks, not optimistic days.

  • FSC-certified oak and walnut, hardwax-oiled
  • Removable, replaceable covers on every upholstered piece
  • Module-level spare parts guaranteed for 15 years
